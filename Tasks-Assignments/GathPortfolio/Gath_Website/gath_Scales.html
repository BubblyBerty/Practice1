These scales and modes were obtained two years ago and their sources were not recorded. However, the majority of them are freely available to be researched from the internet. If any are not found then it is to be assumed that I gave arbitary names to them (colored differently). Modes are specific types of scale that are related to the standard 8 note major scale in Western Music tradition. Notwithstanding this, I have chosen to determine scale sizes ommitting the final note an octave higher than the root note. All scales are based on the Western Music Tradition of the octave being divided into 12 equal notes (Equal Temperment).
