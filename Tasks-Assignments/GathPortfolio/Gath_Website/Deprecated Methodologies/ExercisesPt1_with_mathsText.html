7. Create a definition list with 5 items.

Definitions of Car Engine Components

  1. Cylinder Block - This is the engine's foundation that houses cylinders and crankshaft and provides stability and support.
  2. Cylinders - These are the cylindrical tubes where the air-fuel mixture undergoes combustion to generate power for the engine.
  3. Pistons - Pistons are the moving components inside cylinders that translate the combustion force into crankshaft rotation.
  4. Crankshaft - This is the core component of the engine that converts piston motion into rotational power for the wheels.
  5. Camshaft - This component manages the control of valve opening and closing, ensuring precise synchronisation with engine cycles.
